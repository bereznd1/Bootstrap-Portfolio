About

I am a recent graduate of The College of New Jersey, where I studied Interactive Multimedia & Communications. I am currently working as a Web Designer, but I'm looking to expand my skills and learn more complex development-related knowledge.

When it comes to my personal interests, my biggest passion is Hip-Hop music. I am an avid fan, in particular, of all different forms of Classic Hip-Hop, which I research as much as possible. However, I am getting into modern Hip-Hop as well. I also rap myself, and have released 2 EP's which are available online.

In addition, I host my own radio show called "Don't Sweat The Technique" at WTSR, my former college's radio station, which is dedicated to Hip-Hop music & features a different theme every week.
